About Roomie

Finding a compatible roommate without relying on third-party services can be challenging. Traditional methods often lack security and control over the matching process. This case study explores the development of a peer-to-peer roommate matching platform that empowers users to connect directly, fostering a more transparent and secure roommate search experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform where users can find their perfect roomie without any hassle. We aim to provide a secure and transparent platform for users to connect directly and find their perfect roommate.
